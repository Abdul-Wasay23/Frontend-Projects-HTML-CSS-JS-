About Us

Integrated Mind LLC specializes in providing comprehensive mental health services for individuals struggling with anxiety, depression, substance use disorders, crisis situations, and other psychiatric conditions. Our services include medication management and therapy coordination tailored to meet each individual’s unique needs.

Recognizing the connection between mental and physical health, Integrated Mind also hosts Medical Wellness Days —community outreach events designed to support individuals with chronic medical conditions such as cardiovascular disease, diabetes, cancer, Asthma/Copd, HIV/viral conditions, obesity, smoking cessation, and hyperlipidemia . These clinic days allow participants to receive medication monitoring, laboratory testing, and health education while awaiting follow-up with their primary care provider.

Our Mission

With over 20 years of combined experience in nursing and community outreach, our team is committed to bridging the gap in healthcare access—especially for individuals in rural and underserved areas who face barriers to timely medical care.

Our Vision

To create a world where mental wellness and physical health are seamlessly connected , and where every individual—regardless of circumstance—has access to the care, compassion, and continuity they deserve.
