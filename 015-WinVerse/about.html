About Us

Welcome to WinVerse!

We are Dave and Mark, the directors behind this brand new UK raffle site, and along with our media lead, Heather, we’re excited to bring you fair, fun, and truly Marvalous prize draws.

As a new competition platform, our goal is to grow a strong, friendly community where everyone feels welcome and confident. Every prize is hand-picked, every draw is run with transparency, and we’re always happy to show real winners, go live, or provide proof so you can see we’re 100% genuine.

We’re passionate about creating a place where people can enjoy great prizes, honest competitions, and a trustworthy experience from start to finish.

Thanks for being here — your support helps our Marvalous community grow, and we can’t wait to celebrate our winners (maybe even you!) in the draws to come.
