# Quality First Assistance Group (QFAG)

## Overview
This repository contains the source code for the Quality First Assistance Group (QFAG) website. Quality First Assistance Group is committed to providing necessary resources and tools to make a positive impact on the lives of individuals with disabilities. We assist participants in gaining access to essential programs and services, empowering them to become their own advocates and maintain independence.

The website provides a comprehensive overview of our services, team, career opportunities, and scholarship programs.

## Features
-   **Home Page**: Professional overview of the agency, key highlights, and client testimonials.
-   **About Us**: Deep dive into our mission, vision, and the dedicated leadership team.
-   **Services**: Extensive listing of our core offerings, including Support Coordination and In-Home Support.
-   **Careers**: Current job openings with detailed descriptions and location-specific information.
-   **Scholarship**: Information on our community give-back programs and student sponsorships.
-   **Apply Now**: Integrated application form for potential heart-centered professionals.
-   **Responsive Design**: Fully optimized for a seamless experience on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

## Services Offered
-   **Support Coordination**: Assisting individuals through the process of self-direction.
-   **Community Based Supports**: Empowering individuals to thrive in their community.
-   **Individual Supports**: Tailored care plans for unique needs.
-   **Respite Care**: Providing essential support for families and caregivers.
-   **In-Home Support**: High-quality care and assistance in a comfortable home setting.
-   **Housing Support**: Helping participants find and maintain safe housing.
-   **Transportation Service**: Facilitating access to community and medical appointments.

## Technologies Used
-   **HTML5**
-   **CSS3**: Custom styling, Bootstrap, SlickNav, Fancybox, Animate.css, AOS (Animate On Scroll).
-   **JavaScript**: jQuery, GSAP (GreenSock Animation Platform) for high-end animations, Slick Slider.

## Project Structure
-   `index.html`: Main landing page for the agency.
-   `aboutus.html`: Details about our mission and leadership.
-   `service.html` & `service-detail.html`: Service listings and in-depth descriptions.
-   `career.html`: Career opportunities and agency roles.
-   `apply-now.html`: Professional application form.
-   `scholarship.html`: Community and scholarship program details.
-   `contact-us.html`: Contact information and inquiry form.
-   `css/`: Stylesheets for layout and design.
-   `js/`: Core logic and animation scripts.
-   `images/`: Visual assets and icons.
-   `layout/`: Reusable components like header and footer.

## Usage
To view the website locally:
1.  Clone this repository or download the source code.
2.  Navigate to the project directory.
3.  Open `index.html` in your preferred web browser.
