Cancellation Policy

Appointments at Luxe Skin Aesthetic are in very high demand and it is important to us that we try to accommodate our clients as much as possible. To achieve this, we abide by a strict 24 hour cancellation policy for all appointments whether made via email, in person, online or by telephone. If you do need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please give us 24 hours notice so we can offer you a more convenient time and help you avoid a cancellation fee.

Failure to notify Luxe Skin Aesthetic within 24 hours will result in the loss of the appointment and 100% cancellation fee or loss of a course item. This cancellation policy gives us the time to inform our waiting guests of appointment availability. Our aim is to provide an excellent service level and this policy allows us to achieve this.

A credit card must be taken to secure all appointments. No refunds will be given to cancelled or rescheduled bookings.
