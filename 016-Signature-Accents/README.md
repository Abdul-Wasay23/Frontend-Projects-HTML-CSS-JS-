# Signature-Accents - Premium Wood Decor & Art Platform

## Overview

**Signature-Accents** is a modern and visually stunning e-commerce platform dedicated to artisanal wood decor, home accents, and creative art experiences. The platform showcases a diverse range of products, from precision laser-cut wood pieces to hand-painted items, and offers a unique "Paint Parties" service for art enthusiasts. Designed with a premium aesthetic, it provides a seamless and engaging experience for customers looking to transform their spaces with art.

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Fully optimized for a flawless experience across all devices, from mobile phones to high-resolution desktops.
- **Premium Aesthetics**: A clean, elegant, and modern design featuring glassmorphism elements, vibrant gradients, and professional typography.
- **Dynamic Animations**: Smooth, high-end user interactions powered by GSAP, AOS, and WOW.js.
- **Product Categories**: Easy navigation through diverse collections including Laser Cut Wood Decor, Party Kits, and Hand Painted Items.
- **Paint Parties Integration**: Dedicated section for browsing and signing up for artisanal paint party events.
- **Interactive Gallery**: A rich visual experience featuring high-quality images with FancyBox lightbox integration.
- **Shopping Features**: Comprehensive e-commerce flow including product details, shopping cart, and a streamlined checkout process.
- **User Management**: Integrated account management for registration and secure login.

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: HTML5, Vanilla CSS3, JavaScript
- **Frameworks**: Bootstrap 5, jQuery 3.6.0
- **Animations**: GSAP (GreenSock), ScrollSmoother, AOS (Animate On Scroll), WOW.js, Animate.css
- **Libraries**: Slick Carousel (Sliders), FancyBox (Lightbox), Font Awesome 6.7.2 (Icons)
- **Fonts**: Montserrat, Roboto, Inter (Google Fonts)

## Service Categories

**Laser Cut Wood Decor**: Precision-crafted wooden art, signs, and decorative accents.

**Hand Painted Items**: Unique, artisan-painted home decor and personalized gifts.

**Party Kits**: Complete DIY kits for at-home art projects and celebrations.

**Paint Parties**: Interactive group art experiences and workshop bookings.

## Installation

1. Download or clone this repository to your local machine.
2. Ensure you have a modern web browser installed (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge).
3. Open `index.html` to launch the platform.
4. No server-side installation or database setup is required for the static frontend.
